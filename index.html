Biography

Niklas Wulms is a Dr. rer. medic. student at the Institute for Epidemiology and Social Medicine at the University-Hospital and Wilhelms-University of Münster. He is investigating the MRI data originating from the population-based healthy cohort of the BiDirect study in the BMBF-funded Nachwuchsgruppe ‘Gender differences in the aging brain – patterns, mechanisms and consequences’.
